Narrative

The Global Engagement Office at my university requested a video reflection of my study abroad experiences to help future study abroad students know what to expect. I produced this video in response to their request. It is now part of their resource base for the student body.

My Role

I crafted the script and narrative style, as well as provided all the photos, footage, and narration for this video.

Tools I Used

Made with WeVideo and Canon.

Specifications

  • Convey what the study abroad experience is like to incoming students.
